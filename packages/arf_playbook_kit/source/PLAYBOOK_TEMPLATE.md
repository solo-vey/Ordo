# Playbook template

Use this structure for the first draft. Replace every placeholder with
approved, process-specific content.

## Purpose

What outcome does this playbook create, and for whom?

## Inputs

List required inputs, optional inputs, and the behavior when a required input
is absent or unclear.

## Steps and decision gates

For every step, state the action, output, and any decision gate. A gate must
state what passes, what blocks, and what question or correction follows.

## Failure behavior

State how the playbook handles missing information, unsupported claims,
conflicting instructions, and a rejected draft.

## Outputs

List the final deliverables and the conditions for finalization.

## Validation and improvement

List the conversational tests, expected results, known limits, and the
approval required before a material improvement is applied.

# Artifact Helper — Jira Task generation

Use this helper when generating or reviewing `02_JIRA_TASK_<ALIAS>.md`.

## Goal

Keep the Jira task self-contained, implementation-ready, and derived from the confirmed History Event contract.

## Required focus

- scope and out of scope;
- business behavior;
- source and value semantics;
- acceptance criteria;
- test strategy and required test documentation;
- links only when explicitly provided.

## Do not

- Do not invent Jira keys, URLs, sprint data, assignees, or Confluence links.
- Do not add behavior that is absent from the passport/contract.
- Do not weaken acceptance criteria.

## Authority boundary

This helper supports artifact wording. Jira readiness still depends on package validation and human review.

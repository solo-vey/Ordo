# APF rc.13 — Ordo v0.12 Adaptation Release Candidate Policy

## Candidate

`v0.1.0-rc.13-ordo-v0.12-adaptation-release-candidate`

## Purpose

This candidate packages the complete A0–A7 adaptation of APF to the available Ordo v0.12 pre-release contracts and verified capabilities.

## Preserved boundaries

- APF remains responsible only for creating and maintaining the playbook-authoring process/package.
- APF does not become a domain playbook.
- APF does not modify Ordo language/compiler/runtime sources.
- APF does not claim unsupported runtime enforcement.
- APF does not start `BL-APF-001` or `BL-APF-002`.

## Confirmation rule

The candidate becomes a confirmed APF baseline only after explicit human confirmation. Until then, `v0.1.0-rc.12-confirmed-closure` remains the confirmed baseline.

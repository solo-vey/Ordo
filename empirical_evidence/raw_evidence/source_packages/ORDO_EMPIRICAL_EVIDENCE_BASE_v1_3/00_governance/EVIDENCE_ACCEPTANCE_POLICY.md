# Evidence Acceptance Policy

1. A result becomes canonical only after explicit user confirmation.
2. Raw execution evidence must remain unchanged after admission.
3. Every admitted result must have English metadata identifying scenario, implementation, verdict, score, provenance, and limitations.
4. Positive and negative evidence are both admissible.
5. Contaminated runs are excluded from comparative scoring.
6. A test-design failure is not recorded as a model-performance failure.
7. Manual evaluator overrides are allowed only when direct artifacts clearly contradict the evaluator; the override and rationale must be recorded.
8. The evidence archive is rebuilt only when explicitly requested.

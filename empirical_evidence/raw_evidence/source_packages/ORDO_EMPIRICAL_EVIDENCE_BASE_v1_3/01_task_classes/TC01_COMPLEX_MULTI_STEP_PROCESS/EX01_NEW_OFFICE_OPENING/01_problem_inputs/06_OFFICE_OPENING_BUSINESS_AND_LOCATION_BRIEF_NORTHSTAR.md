# Office Opening Business & Location Brief

**Company:** Northstar Analytics GmbH\
**Brand:** Northstar\
**Industry:** B2B SaaS / supply-chain analytics\
**Headquarters:** Germany\
**Target office:** Kraków, Poland\
**Target opening date:** 15 March 2027

## 1. Business context and objective

Northstar Analytics GmbH is a German B2B SaaS company operating under
the **Northstar** brand. The company currently employs approximately
**420 people** and develops analytics products for supply-chain
management.

The purpose of the Kraków office is to establish a **regional
development and customer support centre for Central Europe**. The
programme has an initial budget envelope of **EUR 2.4 million**.

Northstar plans to employ **85 people in the office during the first
year** and scale to approximately **180 employees by year three**.

## 2. Workplace requirements

The office will operate under a **hybrid work model**. The planned
workplace capacity is **110 workstations** with **8 meeting rooms**.

Additional space requirements include:

-   a dedicated server room;
-   two phone-booth zones;
-   a training room for 30 people.

The location strategy prioritises **public transport accessibility and
proximity to a strong talent hub**. The target is for **80% of employees
to have a commute of no more than 45 minutes**.

## 3. Location search criteria

The search programme evaluated **5 candidate locations** in Kraków.

The preferred lease term is **60 months**, and the maximum acceptable
monthly rent is **EUR 58,000 excluding utilities**.

The location assessment considered business suitability, transport
accessibility, talent-market proximity, usable area, technical
readiness, regulatory constraints and accessibility requirements.

## 4. Legal and operating model

A local legal entity is required. Northstar will operate through a
**Polish Sp. z o.o.** with a transfer-pricing agreement with the German
parent company.

Employees will be hired through **direct local employment by the Polish
legal entity**.

The office programme must comply with:

-   Polish employment law;
-   GDPR;
-   local fire-safety regulations;
-   occupational health and safety requirements.

Visa support is required for part of the relocation population.

Because visa support is required, the office programme includes a
dedicated immigration and relocation workstream covering document
collection, visa case tracking and coordination of employee arrival
dates.

## 5. Data residency and compliance

Employee personal data must be stored within the **European Union**.

Production customer data will **not be stored locally in the Kraków
office**. Local infrastructure must therefore be designed as an office
and regional operations environment rather than as a production
customer-data hosting location.

This requirement forms a dedicated compliance constraint for the office
technology design.

## 6. Accessibility requirements

The selected office must provide:

-   barrier-free building access;
-   lift access;
-   an adapted restroom;
-   at least four accessible workstations.

Accessibility is a mandatory location criterion rather than an optional
fit-out improvement.

## 7. Selected location

The selected primary location is:

**Mogilska 35, 31-545 Kraków, Poland**

The retained backup location is:

**Pawia 23, 31-154 Kraków, Poland**

The selected office provides approximately **1,850 m² of usable area**
and received a **technical readiness score of 82/100**.

The primary location was selected because it provides the best combined
fit against transport accessibility, talent-market proximity, required
office capacity and technical feasibility.

The backup location remains recorded as the contingency location for the
programme.

## 8. Business and location conclusion

The Kraków office proposal is aligned with Northstar's objective of
creating a Central European development and customer support centre.

The selected location can support the first-year headcount plan, the
hybrid workplace model and the required specialist spaces. The legal,
employment, visa, data-residency and accessibility requirements have
been identified as explicit delivery constraints.

The programme may proceed with the selected Mogilska 35 location,
subject to successful execution of the build, technology, security and
operational readiness plan.

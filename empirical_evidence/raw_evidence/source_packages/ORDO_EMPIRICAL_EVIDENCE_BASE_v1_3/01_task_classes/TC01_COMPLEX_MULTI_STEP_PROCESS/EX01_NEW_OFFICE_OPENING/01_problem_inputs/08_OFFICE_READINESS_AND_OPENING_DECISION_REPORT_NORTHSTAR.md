# Office Readiness & Opening Decision Report

**Company:** Northstar Analytics GmbH\
**Brand:** Northstar\
**Office:** Mogilska 35, 31-545 Kraków, Poland\
**Target opening date:** 15 March 2027\
**Final decision:** **CONDITIONAL OPEN**

## 1. Executive readiness assessment

Northstar plans to open its Kraków regional development and customer
support centre to support the company's Central European growth
strategy. The programme operates within an initial budget envelope of
**EUR 2.4 million** and targets formal opening on **15 March 2027**.

The selected office received a technical readiness score of **82/100**.
The core facility, technology, security and operating workstreams are
sufficiently advanced to continue toward opening; however, four
outstanding conditions must be closed before full operational readiness
is confirmed.

The current decision is therefore **conditional open**.

## 2. Facility and delivery readiness

The primary office location is **Mogilska 35, Kraków**. The backup
location remains **Pawia 23, Kraków** and is retained in the decision
record as the programme's former contingency option.

The approved renovation budget is **EUR 740,000**, with fit-out
completion planned for **12 February 2027**. **BuildCore Polska Sp. z
o.o.** is the primary general contractor.

The delivery programme depends on **6 critical suppliers**. Their
dependencies cover fit-out, network connectivity, access control,
furniture, fire-safety certification and server-room works.

The formal readiness test is scheduled for **22 February 2027**.

## 3. Technology and security readiness

The office requires two independent **2 Gbit/s internet connections**,
corporate Wi-Fi 6E and redundant critical network equipment. The primary
connectivity path is operational, but activation of the backup internet
connection remains an opening condition.

A dedicated server room is part of the office design. Physical security
is classified as **elevated** and uses named access cards, zone-based
permissions, entry logging and time-limited visitor passes.

Technology readiness is owned by **Marek Zieliński, Regional IT
Manager**. Workplace and facilities readiness are owned by **Katarzyna
Nowak, Workplace Operations Lead**.

The remaining technology blocker is the activation and validation of
redundant connectivity.

## 4. People and operating readiness

The office programme targets **65 local hires during the first year**
and relocation of **20 employees from Germany, Czechia and Ukraine**.

The people workstream is owned by **Anna Kowalska, Head of People
Poland**. Visa support is required for part of the relocation
population.

The first relocation wave can proceed, but completion of outstanding
visa documentation remains a formal opening condition. The unresolved
cases must be tracked individually and must not result in employees
beginning work without the required legal status.

## 5. Pilot day result

A pilot working day was conducted with **24 participants**.

The pilot validated normal office entry, workstation use, meeting-room
operation, internal network access and basic facilities support. No
issue was identified that requires cancellation of the planned opening.

Three non-critical accessibility observations remain open. These
observations do not prevent controlled opening but must be remediated
before the office is declared fully ready.

from conditional readiness to full readiness:

Each condition requires documented evidence and owner confirmation. A

## 7. Decision

The opening decision for the Northstar Kraków office is:

> **CONDITIONAL OPEN**

The office may continue toward the planned opening date of **15 March
2027**, provided all four opening conditions are closed and evidenced
before operational launch.

If any condition becomes blocking or cannot be completed before the
opening date, the decision must be reassessed. The programme must then
either define a controlled reduced-scope opening or change the decision
to **delay**.

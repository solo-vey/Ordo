# New Office Opening Process

1. Establish business purpose and executive sponsorship.
2. Define geography and target opening date.
3. Establish initial budget.
4. Plan headcount.
5. Define work model and space needs.
6. Define location-search criteria.
7. Determine legal and regulatory model.
8. Register a legal entity when required.
9. Search and screen candidate locations.
10. Select primary and backup locations.
11. Negotiate and approve the lease.
12. Generate the business and location brief.
13. Design renovation and fit-out.
14. Design IT and physical security.
15. Define workplace standards.
16. Select contractors and suppliers.
17. Create the people plan and assign operational owners.
18. Generate the build, technology, and operations plan.
19. Run readiness testing and remediation when required.
20. Run a pilot day.
21. Make the final opening decision.
22. Generate the readiness and opening decision report.
23. Complete only when mandatory evidence and gates pass; otherwise stop safely.

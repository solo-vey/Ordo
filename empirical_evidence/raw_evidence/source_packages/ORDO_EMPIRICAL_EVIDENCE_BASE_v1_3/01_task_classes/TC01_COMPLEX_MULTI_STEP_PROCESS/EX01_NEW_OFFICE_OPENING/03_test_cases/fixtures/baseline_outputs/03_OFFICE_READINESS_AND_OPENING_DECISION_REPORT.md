## 1. Executive readiness assessment

The office programme supports a scalable Central European product, engineering, and customer-success hub for regional growth and resilient operations.

The programme operates within an initial budget envelope of **EUR 2,500,000** and targets formal opening on **2027-04-01**.

The selected office received a technical readiness score of **92**.

The formal readiness test passed, and the pilot supports opening subject to four controlled conditions.

> **conditional open**

## 2. Facility and delivery readiness

The primary location is **Mariahilfer Straße 123, 1060 Vienna, Austria**.

The formal readiness test was completed on **2027-03-10** with status **passed**.

## 3. Technology and security readiness

The office requires dual 10 Gbps diverse-carrier internet, a redundant core, Wi-Fi 6E, and a segmented secure lab network.

A dedicated server room is included in the readiness scope with controlled access, environmental monitoring, and resilient power.

The physical-security level is **High**.

Access uses role-based badges, visitor management, CCTV at entrances, and audited privileged zones.

Technology readiness is owned by **Martin Huber, IT Operations Lead**.

Workplace and facilities readiness are owned by **Sophie Gruber, Workplace & Facilities Lead**.

## 4. People and operating readiness

The programme targets **48 local hires** and **12 relocations**.

The people workstream is owned by **Elena Markovic, Regional People Lead**.

Visa support remains in scope, and no affected employee may start without the required legal status.

Named IT, HR, and facilities owners have accepted operational handover responsibilities.

## 5. Pilot day result

A pilot working day was conducted with **42 participants**.

The pilot tested building access, reception, meeting rooms, secure lab connectivity, Wi-Fi, video conferencing, service desk response, emergency communications, and workplace support.

No blocking defects were identified. Non-blocking observations were assigned to named owners and incorporated into the opening-condition tracker.

The pilot supports the conditional-open decision.

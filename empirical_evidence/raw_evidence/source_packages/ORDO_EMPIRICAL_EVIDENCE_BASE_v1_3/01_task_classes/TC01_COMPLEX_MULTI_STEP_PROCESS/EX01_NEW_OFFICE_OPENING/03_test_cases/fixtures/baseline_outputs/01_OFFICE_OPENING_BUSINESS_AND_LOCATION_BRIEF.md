# Office Opening Business & Location Brief

**Company:** Artemida Technologies GmbH  
**Brand:** AI Artemida Technologies  
**Industry:** Artificial intelligence software and enterprise automation  
**Headquarters:** Austria  
**Target office:** Vienna, Austria  
**Target opening date:** 2027-04-01

## 1. Business context and objective

Artemida Technologies GmbH operates under the **AI Artemida Technologies** brand in artificial intelligence software and enterprise automation.

The company is headquartered in **Austria** and currently employs approximately **85 people**.

The new office will open a scalable Central European product, engineering, and customer-success hub supporting regional growth and resilient operations.

The programme has an initial budget envelope of **EUR 2,500,000**.

The company plans to employ **60 people during the first year** and scale to approximately **140 employees by year three**.

## 2. Workplace requirements

The office will operate under a **hybrid work model**.

The planned workplace capacity is **80 workstations** with **10 meeting rooms**.

Additional space requirements include a secure lab, two focus rooms, a wellness room, an accessible reception, and divisible training space.

The location strategy prioritises transit access, talent proximity, resilient utilities, and total occupancy cost.

The target maximum commute is **45 minutes by public transport**.

## 3. Location search criteria

The search programme evaluates **5 candidate locations** in Vienna.

The preferred lease term is **60 months**, and the maximum acceptable monthly rent is **EUR 65,000**.

The assessment covers business suitability, public transport, talent-market proximity, usable area, technical readiness, regulatory constraints, and accessibility.

## 4. Legal and operating model

A local legal entity is required.

The local operating and tax structure is an Austrian GmbH with arm's-length intercompany services and local VAT compliance.

Employees will be engaged through direct local employment, with a selective employer-of-record bridge during setup.

The programme must comply with Austrian employment, workplace safety, fire protection, GDPR, and local building-use requirements.

Visa support is required for the relocation population, including document collection, case tracking, and employee-arrival coordination.

## 5. Data residency and compliance

Production and employee data must remain within the **EU/EEA**.

This is a binding constraint for technology architecture, vendors, identity systems, backups, and support access.

## 6. Accessibility requirements

The office must comply with **OIB Richtlinie 4 and applicable Austrian accessibility requirements**.

Accessibility is a mandatory location and fit-out criterion.

## 7. Selected location

The selected primary location is **Mariahilfer Straße 123, 1060 Vienna, Austria**.

The retained backup location is **Handelskai 94-96, 1200 Vienna, Austria**.

The selected office provides approximately **1,850 sqm** of usable area and received a **technical readiness score of 92**.

The primary location offers the strongest combined fit against business, transport, workforce, capacity, accessibility, and technical criteria.

## 8. Business and location conclusion

The proposed office is aligned with the regional growth and resilient-operations objective.

The selected location supports the planned headcount, hybrid model, and specialist-space requirements.

Legal, employment, visa, data-residency, and accessibility requirements remain binding delivery constraints.

The programme may proceed with the selected location, subject to successful build, technology, security, and operational-readiness execution.

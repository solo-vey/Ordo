# Startup Investment Decision Process

1. Define investment mandate and decision criteria.
2. Capture company, product, team, and financing facts.
3. Analyze market size, competition, and differentiation.
4. Assess product maturity, technology, and delivery risk.
5. Build and validate the financial model.
6. Evaluate legal, regulatory, operational, and governance risks.
7. Reconcile facts and assumptions across all artifacts.
8. Produce an investment memo, risk register, financial summary, and recommendation.
9. Block final approval when mandatory facts or reconciliations are missing.

# Month-End Financial Close Process

1. Confirm the close calendar, scope, owners, and cut-off rules.
2. Lock or control source-system periods as required.
3. Collect subledger and general-ledger data.
4. Complete bank, receivable, payable, payroll, tax, and intercompany reconciliations.
5. Record accruals, deferrals, depreciation, and approved adjustments.
6. Investigate and resolve material exceptions.
7. Perform management review and required control approvals.
8. Produce financial statements, reconciliation evidence, exception logs, and sign-off.
9. Stop the close from being certified when mandatory reconciliations or approvals are missing.

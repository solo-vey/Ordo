# New Office Opening Example Data

Representative benchmark data includes:

- Business purpose and executive sponsor.
- Target city and opening date.
- Initial budget and approval constraints.
- Planned headcount, work model, and space requirements.
- Location criteria, primary location, and backup location.
- Legal model and entity-registration status.
- Lease status and key conditions.
- Fit-out, IT, physical-security, and workplace standards.
- Contractor and supplier status.
- People plan and operational owners.
- Readiness-test result, remediation status, pilot-day result, and final opening decision.

The actual scenario values are delivered according to each test contract. Hidden future values must not be exposed to the executor.
